Oreo Smores

Ingredients

  • 12 Oreos
  • 12 Marshmallows
  • 4 Hershey Chocolate Bars

Instructions

  1. Preheat the broiler on your oven.
  2. Twist the tops off your Oreo’s. Line a sheet pan with the cookie bottoms, cream side up. Top each of the cookies with a piece of chocolate, followed by a marshmallow.
  3. Place your S'moreos in the oven to broil. Watch them closely as the marshmallow can burn quickly. Broil for about 30 seconds, or until puffed and golden brown.
  4. Once you’ve pulled it out of the oven, top each S’moreo with the remaining cookie tops. Enjoy!

Total Time: 6 MINS
